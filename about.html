Our Achievements

A Small Intro

Our sportswear brand is one the popular brand from last 30 years. Our main products are sports bras, sporty shirts/tank tops, Tracksuits and shorts which are available for males and females. This is a family business with an athletic background of healthy eating. The main problem in starting a family business of sportswear was because there are many athletic people that cannot afford sportswear clothing because they are expensive. Due to subscription offers our demands in stock are increasing day by day. We offer monthly, quarterly and yearly subscription packages which are 25 euros monthly, 45 euros quarterly and 99 euros yearly. Due to situation of COVID 19 people now prefer shopping online instead of going to malls. We have improved our resources to deal with the high demand of customer’s orders from our various stores in Pakistan, South Africa and North America. Our aim is to increase our revenue from 17 million euros in 3 years to 30 million euros in 5 years. We want to fulfil our valuable customers needs as they always wanted but they could not afford. Our target is to become one of the worlds most competitive sportswear company.
